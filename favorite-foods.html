Foods I Love

Here are some of my favorite foods:

  • Pizza - A dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.
  • Sushi - A Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice accompanied by various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.
  • Burgers - A sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun.
  • Pasta - An Italian dish typically made from wheat flour mixed with water or eggs and formed into various shapes, often served with sauce.
